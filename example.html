It All Happened With One Swipe...

You wake one morning to the sound of a familiar ring. It’s your phone notifying you that you have a new match on Tinder! You check to see who it could be. There are actually four new matches, you shrug it off and proceed to have breakfast. There is no need to get too excited it’s only Tinder. Breakfast is way more important anyway because you are very hungry. What do you want to make?



It’s been about an hour after you ate that fish sandwich. You don’t feel so good and you’re having very bad cramps. You look up web md and decide that you have might have food poisoning. After another hour you start throwing up violently, you decide to call the ambulance. They get there just as you take your last breath.

That breakfast was delicious you tell yourself. Just the right amount of lemon in the homemade recipe you got from your mom last Christmas.

Your fruity pebbles were a little bit soggy but they tasted just like you remembered from the days you used to watch Saturday morning cartoons. However, instead of cartoons on the tv, it’s now that orange bloated cheeto head Trump systematically destroying everything good in the world.
